Instructions

Welcome to "Save the Shoreline"!

Your goal is to prevent a coastal city from being submerged due to rising sea levels. Use your Eco Coins wisely to take actions that slow down the sea level rise.

  • Build Sea Wall: Reduces sea level rise by 0.02
    (Cost: 50 Eco Coins)
  • Plant Trees: Reduces sea level rise by 0.01
    (Cost: 30 Eco Coins)
  • Switch to Renewables: Major reduction in sea level rise
    (Cost: 100 Eco Coins)
  • Public Awareness Campaign: Increases eco coin generation efficiency
    (Cost: 20 Eco Coins)
  • Install Solar Panels: Generates Eco Coins over time
    (Cost: 80 Eco Coins)
  • Emergency Flood Drills: Temporarily reduces sea level rise
    (Cost: 40 Eco Coins)
